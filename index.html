Correlations between poverty, lack of healthcare, and obesity

With data from the US census, I looked at the relationship between poverty vs. obesity and lack of healthcare vs. obesity in each state.

Looking first at the relationship between poverty and obesity. There appears to be a correlation between poverty and obesity. It is not the only factor but the highest poverty states are also at the top of the obesity scale.

I also looked at the relationship of obesity to a lack of healthcare. There is not an obvius correlation between the two, the states that have the highest obesity rates fall in the middle of the scale for those that lack healthcare.
